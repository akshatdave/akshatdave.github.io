Comparisons with Ground Truth on Rendered Scenes

PANDORA performs accurate surface estimation, diffuse-specular radiance field separation and illumination estimation from 45 multi-view polarization images of challenging scenes. For instance, PANDORA correctly models the specular compoent when the bust and its base have different specular roughness and can separate out the highly textured diffuse in the globe from sharp specular highlights.
